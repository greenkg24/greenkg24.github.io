About Me

Image

My passions include ultra/trail running, weightlifting, martial arts, DIY wood working and building, guns, and programming. An interesting fact is in 2021 I completed what is known as the “4x4x48 challenge” founded by David Goggins. It is where you run 4 miles every 4 hours for 48 hours straight. It was both mentally and physically challenging and if you know me that is my jam.

I have a yearning to volunteer and help others and have done so throughout my life sporadically, but I have yet to find a regular volunteer opportunity that works for my schedule and aligns with a passion. I have sort of an obsession with personal finances and trying to optimize at every turn, my wife can attest to that.

I have two kids, one boy and one girl and they are my world. My family is the most important thing to me in my life and everything I do is to better ours and their situation. I would not be who I am and accomplished what I have without them in my life. Forever grateful.
